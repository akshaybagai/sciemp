What is a Certificate of Analysis (COA)?

A Certificate of Analysis is a document issued by Quality Assurance that confirms that a product meets its specifications. It includes the product name, lot number, test methods, and results.

How to Search for COA?

Click on the manufacturer's tile above to access their COA search portal. You will typically need the product catalog number and lot/batch number to retrieve your certificate.
