About Us

Our Legacy of Trust Since 1963

Scientific Emporium was founded in 1963 by Mr. J. N. Dhawan with a clear vision to provide quality scientific products and dependable services to educational institutions, laboratories, and research establishments. What began as a small, dedicated effort was formally and legally registered in 1972, marking the establishment of Scientific Emporium as an organized and trusted business entity.

From the very beginning, Mr. J. N. Dhawan aspired to make a respected name in the scientific supply business through honest intentions, ethical practices, and relentless hard work. He believed that long-term success could only be achieved by earning customer trust through consistency, transparency, and quality—values that continue to define the organization.

A Tradition Built on Integrity and Quality

Over the years, Scientific Emporium steadily grew in reputation, becoming known for its reliability, quality products, and customer-focused service approach. The strong foundation laid during its early years has enabled the company to adapt to changing technological and institutional needs while remaining true to its core principles.

Carrying the Legacy Forward

Today, the legacy of Scientific Emporium is proudly carried forward by Mr. Ashwani Dhawan, who has expanded the business and taken it to new heights. By combining modern business practices with the traditional values instilled by the founder, he has enhanced the company's product range, strengthened service support, and extended its reach to a wider network of medical colleges, research laboratories, and educational institutions.

Under his leadership, Scientific Emporium continues to grow while remaining firmly anchored in its founding values of quality, honesty, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be a trusted and preferred supplier of scientific and laboratory equipment by delivering reliable products, ethical business practices, and consistent service excellence.

Our Commitment

We remain committed to upholding the principles established at our inception—ensuring that every product supplied and every service delivered reflects our legacy of trust built since 1963.
